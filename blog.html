Sagittarius Ascendant: Severe Respiratory Issues - What's the Root Cause?

Today, we're examining a detailed analysis of a Sagittarius ascendant native suffering from respiratory problems. Birth details: 04-July-1996, 18:59, Dharapuram (Tamil Nadu).

Chart Structure

  • 4th House: Lungs & Respiratory Tract
  • 8th House: Severe Chronic Illness

The Analysis

Cancer's watery nature links 4th house organs in the 8th house, making breathing problems inevitable under certain dashas.

Bhaskara Astrology (KB) Method

Rahu connects 2/5/8 cuspal sublords, afflicting the respiratory system. Through Bhaskara (KB) cuspal interlink theory, the 8th cusp (Cancer) links with 4th house matters (lungs).

The true source of breathing problems is the 5th house, governing heart and blood circulation. Astrology shows Rahu triggers a heart weakening → breathing obstruction chain reaction.

Summary Conclusion

Rahu (breathing karaka) + 5th house (heart) + 8th house (chronic) combination causes severe affliction. This matches modern medical findings like Pulmonary Edema where heart failure causes fluid buildup in lungs.
